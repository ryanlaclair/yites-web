Yites is holding a remix contest for his new single "Something About You". Winning tracks will be part of a selection of five winners, which will be compiled into a remix album and released worldwide. The remix contest starts on 1/1/2019 and ends 4/1/2019. Instrumental and vocal stems can be found after you register via email below. Good luck all! Registrations will start being accepted 12/10/2018.
